MOST POPULAR

Porto History & Legends - Private Walking Tour


Available in the following languages: Italian, English, Spanish, and Portuguese.


Meet your guide at 9:30 AM or 3:00 PM next to the Trindade metro station (in front of the mural depicting a man with a spray can), at the following address: Rua do Alferes Malheiro 124, Porto.


The tour is private and lasts 3 hours, with the option of a short break. The entire route is mostly flat and accessible to all.

We will visit (from the outside) the following places and attractions:


  • Street of Santa Catarina
  • Bolhao Market
  • Majestic Cafe
  • Avenue of the Aliados
  • Galerias de Paris
  • Lello Bookstore
  • University of Porto
  • Clerigos Tower and Church
  • Museum of Photography
  • Former Jewish Quarter
  • Vitoria Viewpoint
  • Flower Street
  • Sao Bento Train Station
  • Se' do Porto


The tour will end at Porto Cathedral, where the guide will welcome the group and provide recommendations for the best restaurants in the area.


The following experiences can be added to the 3-hour private tour:

- Port wine tasting/Portuguese wines for an additional 20 per person;

- Pastel de Nata workshop for an additional 35 per person;

- Azulejo workshop for an additional 40 per person;


WHAT YOU WILL SEE

Main attractions of Porto and the historic center. It's possible to visit one monument together or go all the way to Ribeira, depending on the tourists' pace.


TOUR TIMES AND DURATION

9:30 AM / 3:30 PM

Duration: 3 hours


ABOUT

There is no age limit. However, for groups over 65, please let me know so we can customize the itinerary with routes best suited to their needs.

The route is mostly flat, but requires a good level of energy and physical condition for walking, especially in summer.

Not suitable for wheelchairs or strollers.

Pets are welcome, provided they are kept on a leash and supervised by their owner.


ADDITIONAL COSTS (Tickets, Food, and Drinks)

Yes, attraction tickets, food, and drinks during breaks are the responsibility of the customer. On the tour, you can visit ONLY the Photography Museum or Porto Cathedral with me at the end of the tour, skipping the line.

30 per person for up to 5 people.

I only accept travelers, but the price would be 60 instead of 30.

Children under 10 are free.

For groups of 5 or more, the 30 per person price obviously varies based on the number of people, so it's customizable and you need to contact me.
